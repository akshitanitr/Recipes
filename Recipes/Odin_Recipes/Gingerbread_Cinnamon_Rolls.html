Gingerbread Cinnamon Rolls

Description

These gingerbread cinnamon rolls are like a cinnabon on steroidsâ€”everyone is going to love them. The rolls are buttery, sugary, and taste like gingerbread.

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Mollases
  • Yeast
  • Kosher Salt
  • Sugar
  • Flour
  • Butter
  • Cooking Spray
  • Eggs

Steps to Follow

  1. Combine warm milk, molasses, and yeast in a small bowl and stir together until combined. Let sit until foamy, about 5 minutes.
  2. Add sugar, salt, and 4 1/2 cups of the flour to the work bowl of a stand mixer fitted with the paddle attachment. Beat on low speed until just combined, about 15 seconds.
  3. Place dough in a large bowl greased with cooking spray and turn to coat. Cover with a clean kitchen towel. Let rise in a warm draft-free place until dough has doubled in size, 1 hour 30 minutes to 2 hours.
  4. Spray a 13x 9-inch pan with cooking spray. Lightly punch down dough. On a lightly floured surface, roll dough into an 18x10-inch rectangle.
  5. Spread 1/2 cup butter over dough for the filling. In a small bowl, stir together brown sugar, molasses, cinnamon, ginger, and cloves until well combined. Sprinkle evenly over butter.
  6. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  7. Bake in the preheated oven, uncovered, until golden brown, 25 to 30 minutes. Let cool in the pan on a wire rack for 15 minutes.
  8. Add cream cheese and 4 tablespoons butter to the bowl of a stand mixer fitted with the paddle attachment; beat on medium speed until smooth, 1 to 2 minutes.
